PadyakCo Member

Previous Next

PadyakCo is an online platform which includes e-commerce, mechanic booking reservation, and blogs for bicycle users. For the e-commerce part, members can view available bicycles and parts in the store, add them to their cart, apply a coupon, checkout by paying through Paypal, and track their order and delivery status. Meanwhile, for the mechanic booking part, members can book a mechanic on their preferred date, track it's status, and view the booking details such as mechanic name and payment breakdown. Lastly, for the blog side, members can view articles made by different authors in the website. This web application is made with the Laravel PHP framework and SQL as the backend. On the other hand, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript, Jqeury, and Ajax are used as the frontend.
